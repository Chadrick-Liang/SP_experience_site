Here's a little titbit about Japanese

The fastest foreign learners of Japanese are ususally Chinese or Korean speakers. This is because the Japanese had adopted Chinese characters for their writing system, meaning most Chinese characters have similar meanings in Japanese.
There are exceptions though. Here's a few.

大丈夫

In Chinese this would mean "husband" or "great man".
In modern Japanese however, it has adopted the meaning of "I am alright" or "It is fine, don't worry".
