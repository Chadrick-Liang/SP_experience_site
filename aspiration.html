I aspire to be a ...

Machine learning engineer

The forefront of Artificial Intelligence!

A machine learning engineer is someone who focuses on researching, building and designing self-running artificial intelligence systems to automate predictive models.

The idea of creating an automated system that is able to classify data and make predictions based on its analysis is fascinating to me.

Software developer

The creators and maintainers of computer systems!

A software engineer is a person who is dedicated to the process of creating, designing, deploying and supporting software.

This profession appeals to me as it involves analysing a problem, coming up with an initial approach and then systematically getting closer to delivering a solution by learning from mistakes made.

Web developer

The designers and creators of websites!

A Web developer is a kind of programmer who specializes in the development of applications relating to the World Wide Web.

I find that this profession is a pleasant fusion of creative and logical thinking as both aesthetics and function matter in this field.
